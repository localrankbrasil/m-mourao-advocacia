## 4. Service Model & Process

### How It Works

**Step 1: Initial Contact**
- Client visits website and reviews firm profile and practice areas
- Client contacts via email or WhatsApp with brief problem description

**Step 2: Initial Assessment**
- Lawyer reviews case basics and initial viability
- Preliminary consultation (may be free or brief)

**Step 3: Fee Agreement**
- Case analysis and fee proposal
- Discussion of costs, timeline, and next steps
- Signature of fee agreement and attorney engagement letter

**Step 4: Service Delivery**
- Ongoing legal work and strategy
- Regular client updates and communication
- Personalized attention and management

**Step 5: Resolution**
- Case closure or ongoing management
- Follow-up support as needed

### Fee Structure

The firm uses **flexible fee arrangements**:
- **Fixed Fees** — For well-defined work (contracts, advisory, planning)
- **Contingency/Success-Based** — For litigation cases where compensation is sought

Fee model depends on:
- Type of matter
- Complexity
- Expected duration
- Likelihood of recovery (for contingency cases)

**Note:** Exact fees are discussed in consultation; not published online (OAB compliance).

## 7. OAB Compliance & Ethical Guidelines

**Relevant Rules:**
- OAB Provimento nº 94/2000 (advertising regulations)
- Código de Ética e Disciplina da OAB

### ✅ What's Allowed

- Display of credentials, education, experience
- Professional achievements and publications
- Client testimonials (in appropriate format)
- Informative content and legal articles
- Practice area descriptions
- Contact information and availability
- Professional photos

### ❌ What's Prohibited

- **Exact fee amounts** (ranges OK; fees discussed in consultation)
- **Guarantees of specific outcomes** ("We guarantee you'll win")
- **Direct comparisons** with other lawyers or firms
- **Exaggerated claims** about expertise or results
- Solicitation or advertising in certain restricted formats
- Use of testimonials that are coerced or fake

### ✓ Best Practices

- Keep all content truthful and verifiable
- Update credentials as they change
- Avoid sensationalism or dramatization
- Professional, dignified tone
- Clear distinction between information and advertising
- Proper attribution for published work
